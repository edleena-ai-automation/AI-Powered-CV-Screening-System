# AI-Powered-CV-Screening-System

HR teams waste 5-6 hours daily reading CVs manually.
This system does it automatically in minutes — using AI.

## ❌ Problem

- HR teams manually read 50-100 CVs per job
- Good candidates get missed due to human fatigue  
- Slow replies to applicants — they lose interest
- Interview scheduling takes days of back-and-forth emails
- No proper tracking of who applied and what happened

## ✅ My Solution — How It Works

Step 1: Applicant fills Google Form and uploads their CV
Step 2: CV automatically saves to Google Drive
Step 3: System extracts all text from the CV
Step 4: OpenAI reads the CV and checks if candidate fits the job
Step 5: If YES — candidate data saves to Google Sheets
Step 6: Shortlisted candidate gets an email automatically
Step 7: Interview gets scheduled on Google Calendar — automatically

## 🔧 Tools Used

- n8n — main automation tool
- Google Forms — to collect CV and applicant info
- Google Drive — to store CV files
- OpenAI — to read and analyze CVs with AI
- Google Sheets — to track shortlisted candidates
- Gmail — to email selected candidates
- Google Calendar — to schedule interviews
